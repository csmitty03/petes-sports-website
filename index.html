Our Story

A London institution since 1978.

Pete's Sports was established in London, Ontario in 1978 and has been providing customers with top quality sales and service for nearly 50 years.

We specialize in hockey, baseball and more — offering professional embroidery and cresting, and full team outfitting for athletes at every level.
